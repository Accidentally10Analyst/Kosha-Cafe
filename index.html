About Kosha Cafe

Founded in 2018, Kosha Cafe began as a dream to create a space where community, quality, and comfort converge. Our name "Kosha" means "treasure" in Sanskrit, reflecting our belief that every moment shared over coffee is precious.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms around the world, ensuring fair trade practices and exceptional quality. Our skilled baristas craft each cup with precision and passion, creating an experience that goes beyond just great coffee.

More than just a cafe, we're a gathering place for students, professionals, artists, and dreamers. Our cozy atmosphere, free WiFi, and comfortable seating make us the perfect spot for work, study, or simply enjoying life's simple pleasures.
